## Voice & Tone

### Do Say
- "We build scalable SaaS products"
- "High-performance websites"
- "Founder-level clarity"
- "We ship features, not excuses"
- "Clean, maintainable code"

### Don't Say

- "Rockstar" or "ninja" developers
- "Cutting-edge" or "disruptive"
- "Revolutionary" or hyperbolic claims
